At least a dozen mid-stage Indian SaaS companies have moved off pure seat-based pricing in the last six months, shifting to usage, outcomes, or hybrid models.

What happened: AI features collapsed the link between headcount and value. When one operator with a copilot does the work of four seats, seat pricing punishes your best customers.

Why it matters: Indian SaaS sells disproportionately to cost-sensitive mid-market buyers abroad. Get the new pricing wrong in either direction and you either bleed margin or bleed logos.

Our read: nobody has solved this, including the companies announcing it confidently. The honest version is that everyone is running pricing experiments in production and calling it strategy.
